SHOW COMMITTEE APPLICATIONS FOR 2019 ARE NOW OPEN!!!

We are looking for committed, independent and responsible people who are passionate about dance and want to contribute to Dance Club's largest project of the year! If you have always wanted to get more involved with Dance Club, expand your social network of like-minded dancers, and leave LSE having contributed to something tangible, this is your opportunity. The positions we are filling are: Assistant Show Director Programs Manager Projects Manager Logistics Manager Please find the application form here: Here The form contains an outline of what the roles will involve, and you are able to apply for more than one role. If you intend to apply for more than one position, please submit only one application, and select the roles you wish to apply for. If you have any questions about the show committee, do approach any of the committee members! Some of us (Joanne, Maria and Daphne) were on the show committee in 2017/2018, so we will be able to answer any specific questions on the individual roles. Applications will close on 30th November!
